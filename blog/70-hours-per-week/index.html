70 Hours per Week

November 1, 2023

Old men and their advise. Sometimes I wonder if you should take any of these old men seriously. If you don’t know what I am talking about, Infosys founder Narayana Murthy recently said that young Indians must work 70 hours a week. 70 hours a week. What a phenomenal advice from a capitalist.

His argument is, if you want India to realize its economic potential every young Indian must say: “This is my country. I want to work 70 hours a week.” 1

Well, you do not need to work 70 hours to realize its economic potential.2 Maybe he forgot the part where the quality of life also plays a crucial part in economic development? Maybe higher wages would help? I don’t know, I am no expert. But let’s assume we do for the sake of argument.

If we work five days, we have 24 * 5 = 120 hours minus the 70 hours for work. But what will you do with the 50 hours that is left anyway?

– Sleeping? On average an adult needs a minimum of 8 hours per day, so for 5 days it would be 40 hours 30 hours.

  • cause sometimes it’s worse and sometimes you sleep great. Let’s just say a humble 6 hours per day.

– Commuting? Maybe just work for 2 hours more while stuck in Bangalore traffic? Are you hinting on that Mr. Murthy?

– Attire and Grooming? Maybe you are so engrossed at work that you forgot to brush your teeth? Maybe you accidentally wore smelly socks and embarrass yourself, or perhaps forgot what you were thinking because you did not eat, and you were late for work and you were in a hurry? Or maybe you just need 6 hours to avoid such err. To be on the safer side, you know?

– Eating? 30 mins per meal and 3 meals a day that’s 1.5 hours/day, so overall 7.5 hours. What a waste of time. I’d rather just swallow my pride and work inefficiently, because obviously quantity matters over quality?

Add it all up, and yet there are other things far more important than work.

Oh, and really love the classic Nationalist angle to make a statement.

For the sake of India, you cannot work 14 hours a day? Oh come on?! Where’s all the energy draining? Don’t you tell me you are working two jobs?? Oh right, that’s called moonlighting. Don’t do that, that’s unethical! You can only slog for us, because we are a big fucking company and you are supposed to do whatever the fuck we tell you to.

Wait, why are you wasting time in reading this blog? What’s wrong with you?! Didn’t you read the title?! Get back to work now!

I have immense respect for what the man has built, however statements like these make me question his intelligence.
